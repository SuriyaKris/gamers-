THE WALKING DEAD

The Walking Dead video game is an episodic video game based on Robert Kirkman's The Walking Dead graphic novel series.

The world has turned into chaos with no explanation or reason. The dead walk the eartht to send the living down to hell. One man struggle to survive and do what is right in this world gone mad. Can he make the best of his new life?
